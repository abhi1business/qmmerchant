Payment Terms

Important Information

All our payment, loading, and delivery conditions are designed to ensure smooth international trade operations. We offer flexible terms to accommodate various business requirements while maintaining security and transparency in all transactions.

Payment Conditions

We offer multiple secure payment options to facilitate international trade with flexibility and security.

  • 80% Advance & balance on loading FOR

    80% payment in advance, remaining 20% upon loading of goods

  • 80% Advance & balance on delivery at Port FOB

    80% advance payment, balance upon delivery at port of shipment

  • 80% Advance & balance against scan copy of BL and documents CIF/CNF

    Balance payment against scanned Bill of Lading and shipping documents

  • 100% at sight LC from prime international bank CIF/CNF

    Full payment through Letter of Credit from reputable international banks

Loading Conditions

Efficient and secure loading processes to ensure your goods are handled with care and precision.

  • Truckload

    Full truckload shipments for bulk orders with optimized logistics

  • Container (Factory Stuffing)

    Direct container stuffing at factory premises for maximum security and efficiency

Delivery Conditions

Comprehensive delivery options tailored to meet your specific logistical requirements.

  • FOR (Free on Rail)

    Delivery to specified railway station, all charges paid by seller until rail point

  • FOB (Free on Board)

    Goods delivered on board the vessel at port of shipment, all charges until loading

  • CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight)

    Includes cost of goods, marine insurance, and freight to destination port

  • CNF (Cost & Freight)

    Includes cost of goods and freight to destination port (insurance excluded)
