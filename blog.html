The Art of Premium Basmati Rice

Basmati rice from India is renowned worldwide for its distinctive aroma, long grains, and delicate flavor. Our premium Basmati undergoes a meticulous process to ensure it meets the highest international standards.

Quality Parameters We Maintain:
Grain Length

Minimum 8.3mm grain length with slender appearance

Aroma

Natural fragrance characteristic of traditional Basmati

Moisture Content

Maximum 14% moisture for optimal shelf life

Broken Grains

Less than 2% broken grains in premium quality

Aging Process:

Our Basmati rice is carefully aged for 1-2 years to enhance its natural fragrance and ensure non-sticky, separate grains when cooked. This traditional aging process is what sets authentic Basmati apart.

Did You Know? Authentic Basmati rice grains elongate to almost twice their original length when cooked.
